Aeonid Colossus

"Hero-Breaker"

Origin

Forged by Hephaestus himself using the essence of fallen heroes, this construct is a guardian designed to protect the most valuable divine treasures from getting into the hands of mortals.

Its eyes gleam with the wisdom of Odysseus, its arms bear the strength of Heracles, and its heart burns with the divine flame of Prometheus.

Divine Origin

The Colossus draws its power from these sacred artifacts:

Nemean Pelt

Torn from the mighty Nemean Lion by Heracles himself, this golden fur renders the Colossus nearly invulnerable to mortal weapons.

◼ Base AC 19
◼ Can be torn off (AC 23, 50 HP)
◼ Removed: AC becomes 16
◼ Loses physical damage resistance

Winged Sandals

The same sandals that carried Perseus to victory against Medusa now grant the Colossus mastery over the skies.

◼ 60ft flying speed
◼ Destroyable (AC 23, 50 HP)
◼ Removal disables flight actions

Heroic Weapons

Each weapon carries the essence of its original wielder, from Perseus's sword to Odysseus's bow.

◼ Destroy weapons (AC 23, 50 HP each)
◼ Each destroyed: -2 to attack rolls
◼ Lose special attack properties

Tactical Overview

The Colossus employs different strategies based on its opponents:

  • Against Melee Fighters: Uses flight to maintain distance while pelting with arrows
  • Against Spellcasters: Closes distance rapidly using Winged Ascent, focuses on disrupting concentration
  • When Surrounded: Employs Hero's Legacy in cone formation, follows with Labor of Heracles
"Its footsteps echo with the might of Olympus."
- Inscription on the Colossus's soles

Amalgam of Heroes. The colossus is immune to effects that would reduce its speed, immobilize specific body parts, or cause limb-specific penalties. Disarming attacks fail automatically.
Divine Ichor. At the start of each turn, the colossus regains 20 hit points unless it has taken lightning damage since its last turn.
Golden Apple of Discord (1/Day). As an action, the colossus can target one creature within 60 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw or be compelled to attack the nearest creature for 1 minute. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns.

Actions

Multiattack. The colossus makes three weapon attacks.
Sword of Perseus. Melee Weapon Attack: +14 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 3d8 + 8 slashing damage. On a critical hit, the target must succeed on a DC 22 Constitution saving throw or be petrified.
Bow of Odysseus. Ranged Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 2d8 + 4 piercing damage. This attack ignores half and three-quarters cover.
Hero's Legacy (Recharge 5-6). The colossus channels the power of ancient heroes, choosing one of the following effects:
• Acid Breath: The colossus exhales acid in a 60-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw, taking 8d8 acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
• Arrow Storm: The colossus fires six arrows at up to six targets within 600 feet. Each target must make a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d8 + 4 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Legendary Actions (3/Round)

Winged Ascent. The colossus flies up to 60 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.
Labor of Heracles (Costs 2 Actions). The colossus chooses one of the following effects:
• Charging Strike: The colossus moves up to its speed in a straight line and makes one melee attack. If the attack hits, it deals an extra 4d10 damage.
• Ground Slam: Each creature within 15 feet must succeed on a DC 19 Strength saving throw or take 4d10 + 8 bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone.
⚡ Lightning Vulnerability: The colossus takes 200% more damage from lightning effects and must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of its next turn whenever it takes lightning damage.
